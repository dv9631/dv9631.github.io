Risk managment

  • Clause 3. Requirements of risk managment process

    • 3.1 risk managment process requirments

      • 3.1.1 deocument hazards associated with a medical device
      • 3.1.2 estimate and evaluate associated risks
      • 3.1.3 control the risk
      • 3.1.4 moitor effectiveness of the control
      • 3.1.5 elements: Risk analysis| Risk evaluation| risk control|production and post production information|
      • 3.1.6 documented product realisation process
    • 3.2 managemnt responsibility requirments
      • 3.2.1 person responsible for risk managemnt
      • 3.2.2 define and document policy for risk managment
      • 3.2.3 define criteria for risk acceptability
      1. Risk managment plan
        • establish and document a risk management plan for risk management process
          • scope of planned activities
          • identifying and describing medical device and the life cycle phases
          • assignment of responsibilities and authorities
          • review requirments
          • risk acceptability criteria: manufacturers policy
          • verification activities
          • collection of production and post production information
      2. Risk managment file
        • traceability for each identified hazard
        • risk analysis
        • risk evaluation
        • implementation and verification of risk control measures
        • residual risk acceptability and asesment

      Clause: 4 risk analysis

      1. Risk analysis process
        • implementation of the planned risk analysis activities and the results of risk analysis shall be recorded in the risk managment file
        • description and identification of the device analysed
        • person and organisation carrying out the analysis
        • scope and date of the analysis
      2. intended use and identification of characteristics related to safety of the medical device
        • document intended use
        • document forseable misuse
        • document quantitative and qualitative characteristics that could affect safety of the device,and teir defined limits
        • maintained in risk managment file
      3. identification of hazards
        • known and forseable hazards associated with device in normal and fault conditions
      4. estimation of risk for each hazardous situation
        • reasonably forseable sequences or combination of events that can result in hazardous situation shall be considered and resulting hazardous situation shall be recorded
        • estimate risk using available information for each hazardous situation
        • if probability of occurence of harm cannot be estimated,possible consequences shall be listed
        • system used for qualitative or quantitative categorisation of probability of occurence of harm or severity of harm shall be recoded
        • information or data for estimating risk can be obtained from:
          • published standards
          • scientific technical data
          • field data from similar medical devices already in use ,including published reported incidents
          • usability tests employing typical users
          • clinical evidence
          • results of approperiate investigations
          • expert opinion
          • external quality assesment schemes

      Clause 5 : Risk evaluation

      • guidance for risk acceptability
      • each hazard identified, decide if risk reduction is required or not

      Clause 6 : Risk control

      1. risk reduction
      2. risk control option analysis
        • identify risk control measures approperiate for reducing risk
          • inherent safety by design
          • protective measure in the medical device itself or in manufacturing process
          • information for safety
        • measure selected must be recorded in risk managment file
        • if risk reduction is not practicable, risk benefit analysis to be performed
      3. implementation of risk control measures
        • implementation of each risk control measure must be verified and recorded
        • effectiveness verified
      4. Residual risk evaluation
        • after risk control measure applied,residual risk evaluated
        • residual risk not aceptable,further risk control measures must apply
        • for residual risks acceptable,manufacturer to decide which to disclose and what information is necessary to include in documents
      5. risk benefit analysis
        • not acceptable residual risk,further risk control is not practicable,gather and review data and literature to determine if intended use otweighs residual risk
        • if evidence does not support conclusion that medical benefits outweigh residual risk,then risk remains unacceptable
        • if benefit outweighs risk, information for safety to be disclosed
      6. risk arising from risk control measures
        • introduction of new hazards,hazardous situation
        • risk control measure affects estimated risk for previous identified hazardous situation
      7. completeness of risk control
        • risks from all identified hazardous situations haave been considered

      Clause 7 : Evaluation of overall residual risk

      • overall residual risk posed by the medical device is acceptable using the criteria defined
      • gather and review data and literature to determine if medical benefits of intended use outweigh the overall residual risk

      Clause 8: Risk managemnt report

      • review of risk managment process
      • risk managemnt plan has been approperiately implemented
      • overal residual risk is acceptable
      • approperiate methods are in place to obtain relevant production and post production information
      • rewiew recorded as risk managment report and included in risk managment file

      Clause 9:Production and post production information

      • establish system to collect and review information about the medical device in production and post production phase
      • information generated by operator,user,those accountable for installation use and maintenance is collected and processed
      • publicly available information about similar medical devices
      • if previously unrecognised hazards or hazardous situvations are present
      • estimared risk arising from a hazardous situvation is/are no longer acceptable
      • impact on previously implemented risk managment activities shall be evaluated and fed back as an input to the risk managment process
      • review of risk managment file conducted, if potential of residual risk or its acceptability has changed, the impact on the previously implemented risk control measure must be evaluated
